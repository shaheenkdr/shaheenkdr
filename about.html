About


My name is Shaheen, I work as an AI Lead at Mashinga. Where I am assigned to train, optimize and scale state of the art Deep Learning based Speech Recognition & NLP models. I'm from India, and I currently live in Hyderabad. My main interests consistently evolve around Deep Learning for Signal Processing & NLP. I also am passionate about Cryptography and Linear Algebra. I maintain a blog where I write about topics that excites me in Speech and NLP.
Apart from that I love macro, travel & wildlife photograpy for leisure .

Research

This section is a repository to keep track of my research interests. As I am passionate about research in Automated Speech Recognition and Natural Language Processing(NLP), I keep a curated list of my favourite research papers here. The list is categorized with various tags, making it easier for anyone to refer.

Resources

This section is a repository to keep track of all the pre-trained DNN models, datasets, tools and libraries which I found to be very interesting. The repository has a curated list of all datasets for ASR & NLP tasks, also pre-trained DNN models that simplifies and saves the pain & cost of training models, also openly licensed libraries & tools that are worth trying out.
